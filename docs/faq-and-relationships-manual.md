# A Quick Guide to The Forum and Relationship Principles Pages

This guide walks you through the features of The Forum (formerly FAQ) and the editing capabilities of the Relationship Principles page, designed for Mentors.

---

## The Forum (`/faq`)

The Forum is our community knowledge base. All questions and their corresponding answers are displayed in an open, side-by-side format for quick scanning.

- **Search:** Use the search bar at the top to filter all questions and answers by keyword.
- **Direct Linking:** Each question has a unique ID in the URL (e.g., `/faq#faq-123`), making it easy to link directly to a specific entry.
- **Quick Actions:** Buttons at the top allow you to "Ask Ed," "Ask a Mentor," or "Ask the AI Chief" for immediate support.

### Editing Questions and Answers

As a mentor, you have full control over the content. The "Edit" (pencil) and "Delete" (trash) icons are visible to you on questions and answers.

- **To Edit:** Click the "Edit" icon next to a question or an existing answer.
- **Formatting Toolbar:** A simple toolbar with **Bold**, *Italic*, and <u>Underline</u> buttons is available. Highlight text and click a button to apply formatting.
- **Saving:** You have two options for saving an answer:
    1.  **Save:** Updates the content without notifying the user.
    2.  **Save & Notify:** Updates the content **and** sends an email to the person who asked the question with a direct link to the answer.

### The Mentor Dashboard

Your dashboard provides a focused view of new activity:

- **Pending Forum Entries:** This list **only** shows questions that have zero answers. It clearly indicates if a question was directed to "Ed," "a Mentor," or "a Chief." Once any mentor provides feedback, the question is automatically moved to the public Forum and removed from this list.
- **Manual Forum Entry:** For questions that come from external sources (like emails), use the **Ed's Manual Forum Entry** form. This lets you input a contributor's name, the question, and your answer all at once.

---

## Relationship Principles (`/relationships`)

As a Mentor, you also have editing control over the Relationship Principles page.

### How to Edit

1.  **Enter Edit Mode:** Navigate to the Relationship Principles page. A button labeled **"Edit Page"** appears in the bottom-right corner.
2.  **Edit Content:** Click on any title or content block to change the text. You can also update the image path.
3.  **Manage Sections:** You can reorder sections using the Up/Down arrows, add new sections at the bottom, or remove existing ones.
4.  **Save or Cancel:** Click **"Save Changes"** to make your edits live, or **"Cancel"** to discard them.
