# A Quick Guide to the FAQ and Relationships Pages

This guide walks you through the updated features of the FAQ system and the editing capabilities of the Relationships page, designed for Mentors.

---

## The Main FAQ Page (`/faq`)

We've moved away from the old accordion-style view. Now, all questions and their corresponding answers are displayed in an open, side-by-side format. This allows you to quickly scan all entries without needing to click to expand each one.

- **Search:** Use the search bar at the top to filter all questions and answers by keyword.
- **Direct Linking:** Each question now has a unique ID in the URL (e.g., `/faq#faq-123`), making it easy to link directly to a specific entry.

### Editing Questions and Answers

As a mentor, you have full control over the content. The "Edit" (pencil) and "Delete" (trash) icons are always visible to you on questions and answers.

- **To Edit:** Simply click the "Edit" (pencil) icon next to a question or an existing answer. This will turn the text into an editable field.
- **Formatting Toolbar:** A simple toolbar with **Bold**, *Italic*, and <u>Underline</u> buttons will appear above the text box. Highlight any text and click a button to apply formatting.
- **Saving:** Once you're done editing, click "Save" to update the content.

### Replying and Notifying Users

When an answer is edited, you now have two options for saving:

1.  **Save:** This button updates the answer content without notifying the user. Use this for minor corrections or updates.
2.  **Save & Notify:** This button saves your changes **and** sends an email to the person who originally asked the question. The email includes a direct link back to the FAQ entry so they can see your answer.

### Deleting Content

To keep the FAQ clean and relevant, you can delete content:

- **Delete an Answer:** Click the "Delete" (trash) icon next to any answer to remove just that specific reply.
- **Delete an Entire FAQ Entry:** Click the "Delete" (trash) icon on the *question* card. This will permanently remove the question and **all** of its associated answers. A confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletions.

### The Mentor Dashboard

Your dashboard is now smarter:

- **Pending FAQs:** The "Pending FAQ" list now **only** shows questions that have zero answers. Once any mentor provides feedback, the question is automatically removed from this list.
- **Quick Link:** A "Go to FAQ Page" button has been added for easy navigation to the main FAQ view.

### Adding FAQs Manually

For questions that come from external sources (like emails), you can use the **Manual FAQ Entry** form on your Mentor Dashboard.

- This form lets you input a contributor's name, the question, and your answer all at once.
- The same formatting toolbar is available here for both the question and answer fields, so you can style the text as needed.

---

## Editing the Relationships Page (`/relationships`)

As a Mentor, you also have editing control over the Relationships page.

### How to Edit

1.  **Enter Edit Mode:** Navigate to the Relationships page. A button labeled **"Edit Page"** will appear in the bottom-right corner. Click it to enable editing.
2.  **Edit Content:**
    *   Once in edit mode, you can directly click on any title or content block to change the text.
    *   You can also update the image path for each section by editing the URL in the input field that appears below the image.
3.  **Add a New Section:** Scroll to the very bottom of the page. Click the **"Add New Section"** button to create a new, blank principle block. You can then fill in the title, content, and image URL.
4.  **Remove a Section:** In edit mode, each section will have a **"Remove Section"** button. Clicking this will delete the entire principle block.
5.  **Save or Cancel:**
    *   When you are finished with your edits, click the **"Save"** button in the bottom-right corner to make your changes live.
    *   If you want to discard your changes, click the **"Cancel"** button.
